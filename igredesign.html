The Problem

I love Instagram. Any function that has ever been released, I have tried and I was never disappointed. The release of effects has really changed the way users use the app. I noticed a dramatic increase in stories posted because there is just so much more creative freedom for the users. However, the effects that I have saved are only from my friends’ stories. There is also a gallery that contains all the effects for users to search and discover but the gallery is very hard to find and doesn’t allow users to easily find what they are looking for.





User Research

For my user research, I sent out surveys and conducted in-person observations on 10 participants. I reason why I decided to use both of these methods is that surveys are a great way to gather a large amount of data but also the questions prompt them areas of concern that they could miss during an in-person observation. For in-person observations, I am able to get a clear picture of the user journey and emotions and to account for things that they didn’t express on the survey.

Key Findings

People use effects to make their stories look more interesting/2 out of 5 people don’t know where the gallery is located/People who know where it is, don’t browse it often/The function is supposed to be instant but picking filters takes time

Users' Goals




Design Goals: How Might We?

How might we improve the easiness of access to the effects gallery?/How might we improve the discoverability of effects?/How might we improve the function and process?/How might we improve the discoverability of effects while maintaining the level of account interaction?





Redesigning

Problem 1

Location of effects gallery has low discoverability

People sometimes feel like they are missing out when they don’t know where the effects gallery is located or can’t find an effect for their story.




Solution

Relocate the effects gallery to increase discoverability
